About

Hello there, and welcome to my design portfolio! My name is Ditte, and I am a passionate multimedia design student at UCL in Odense, Denmark. I love diving into a brand's visuals and making memorable design experiences that stand out from the crowd and tells a story. No matter if it may be regarding optimizing the design and user-friendliness of a website, making engaging content to their social media accounts or creating captivating graphic content to either digital or printable use.

I also focus on adding a touch of girliness and cuteness while keeping it calming and professional to look at, and while also challenging my creativity and expanding my horizon in the world of UX, UI and digital design.

Through my developed skills and my passions, I hope to pique an interest about working together to hopefully elevate the content of your skincare brand to new heights! I will do my outmost best to bring any ideas or thoughts you may have to life and to make it match your brand the best possible way as well as making it resonate with your target group.

Thank you for visiting my portfolio. I hope you enjoy exploring it as much as I enjoyed creating it.
